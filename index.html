UPSC Civil Services Examination

The UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) is one of the most prestigious and competitive exams in India. It is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) to recruit candidates for various administrative positions in the Indian Civil Services, including the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), Indian Police Service (IPS), and Indian Foreign Service (IFS), among others.

The UPSC CSE consists of three stages: the Preliminary Examination (Prelims), the Main Examination (Mains), and the Personality Test (Interview). It tests candidates on a wide range of subjects, including general studies, aptitude, and an optional subject chosen by the candidate.

The exam is known for its comprehensive nature and its ability to assess candidates' knowledge, analytical skills, and suitability for the civil services. Successfully clearing the UPSC CSE opens doors to a prestigious career in public service, where individuals have the opportunity to serve the nation and make a positive impact on society.
